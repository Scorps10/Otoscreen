Step 2 of 3 — Phone Placement
How to Hold the Phone
The instructions differ slightly between iPhone and Android. Find your phone below.
📱 iPhone
1
Hold portrait, screen facing you. Rotate counter-clockwise 90° — bottom edge now on the right.
2
Bring that right edge to the child's ear. Speaker is at the top of the edge — toward the face. Screen faces you (the parent).
3
Tilt the top of the phone toward the back of the head — this rotates the speaker inward along the canal axis. Most of the phone sits behind the ear.

1

3
Tilt the top of the phone toward the back of the head — this rotates the speaker inward along the canal axis. Most of the phone sits behind the ear.
