⚠️ Not a Medical Device
OtoScreen is an acoustic screening aid for parental awareness only. It is not a certified medical device and cannot diagnose, treat, or replace clinical evaluation. Results should never be used as the sole basis for medical decisions.

Middle ear conditions in children require diagnosis by a licensed healthcare provider. If you are concerned about your child's hearing or ear health, consult a pediatrician or ENT specialist.
