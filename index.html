How It Works 🔬

Principle: A healthy eardrum reflects sound cleanly. Fluid behind the eardrum dampens vibration — less energy returns, at a lower frequency. This is the basis of clinical tympanometry.

Volume gate (v3): Before every session, the app emits a reference tone and measures its own output via the mic. It guides you to set the phone volume to a safe level for a child's ear before the real test begins. The chirp is also hard-limited to 15% of max signal amplitude in code.

Chirp: 500–4000 Hz log sweep, 50ms, 8 sweeps averaged.

Limitations: Not clinical tympanometry. No ear canal seal. Results are directional only.

⚠️ Not a medical device. OtoScreen is an acoustic screening aid for parental awareness only. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
