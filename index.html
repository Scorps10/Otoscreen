How It Works 🔬
The science behind OtoScreen

The principle: A healthy eardrum freely vibrates and reflects sound energy. When fluid builds up behind the eardrum (middle-ear effusion / "glue ear"), it dampens vibration — the eardrum absorbs more sound and reflects less, and at a lower resonant frequency.

What we emit: A 500–4000 Hz logarithmic chirp sweep (~80ms) played through the phone speaker held 2–3 cm from the ear canal opening.

What we measure:
• Return amplitude — how much sound energy bounces back
• Peak resonant frequency — fluid shifts this lower (~800–1200 Hz vs ~2000+ Hz healthy)
• Left/right asymmetry — unilateral fluid causes detectable imbalance

Limitations: This is NOT clinical tympanometry. No ear canal seal is used. Results are directional indicators, not diagnoses. Ear wax, motion artifacts, and room noise all affect readings.

⚠️ Not a medical device. OtoScreen is an acoustic screening aid for parental awareness only. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
